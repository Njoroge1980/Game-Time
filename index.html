Fun and Games with Ping Pong

Welcome! This is a simple game filled with loads of fun.

Rules of Engagement

  1. Enter a number, any number, but preferably a big number.
  2. Ping pong will then count up to your number from 1 except when:
  • Number is divisible by 3 it becomes 'ping'
  • Number is divisible by 5 it becomes 'pong'
  • Number is divisible by 15, it becomes 'pingpong
    • '

Type your number in the box provided and submit it
